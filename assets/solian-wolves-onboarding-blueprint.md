## 2. PHASE 1: THE SURGICAL AUDIT METHODOLOGY
The foundation of every Solian Wolves project is the **Surgical Audit**. We do not build on top of broken foundations. Before we write a single line of production code, we must understand the "Technical Debt" and the "Friction Points" currently holding you back.

### 2.1 Core Web Vitals (CWV) & Performance Deep-Dive
Google's Core Web Vitals are no longer "optional" suggestions—they are the gatekeepers of your organic traffic and user retention. 

#### Largest Contentful Paint (LCP)
We analyze your LCP to ensure the largest visible element (usually your hero image or headline) renders in under **1.2 seconds**. We go beyond simple image compression; we look at:
- **Server Response Times (TTFB)**: Most agencies ignore back-end latency. We optimize it.
- **Resource Load Delay**: We prioritize the critical path so the most important pixels hit the screen first.
- **Client-side Rendering Bottlenecks**: If you're using a heavy JavaScript framework (like React or Vue), we ensure hydration doesn't block the visual paint.

#### Interaction to Next Paint (INP)
This is the new gold standard for responsiveness. When a user clicks your "Add to Cart" button, does the site freeze for 300ms while processing? That's a lost sale. We audit your main-thread execution using Chrome DevTools to identify long tasks and yield execution to the browser, ensuring an INP of under **200ms**.

#### Cumulative Layout Shift (CLS)
Nothing screams "unprofessional" like a button that moves right before a user clicks it. We audit for layout stability, ensuring all media elements have reserved aspect ratios and font-loading doesn't cause "flashes of unstyled text" (FOUT).

### 2.2 Technical Debt & Infrastructure Analysis
Most websites are built like Jenga towers—one more plugin and the whole thing collapses.
- **Third-Party Script Audit**: We find the "Ghost Scripts"—tracking pixels for long-dead marketing campaigns that are still hogging your bandwidth.
- **Database Scalability**: If you're running on a legacy CMS, we check your query efficiency. Slow database lookups are the silent killers of conversion.
- **API & Integration Health**: We verify that your CRM, ERP, and payment gateways are communicating via optimized, secure webhooks rather than slow polling.

### 2.3 UX Friction & Psychology Audit
We use heatmaps and session recordings (Honeypot, Hotjar, or custom tracking) to see where your users are getting stuck.
- **The "Three-Click" Rule Re-imagined**: Can a user get from "Curious" to "Check Out" in under 15 seconds?
- **Cognitive Load Analysis**: Are you asking for too much information in your forms? Is there too much "noise" on your product pages?
- **Mobile-First UX**: 70%+ of your traffic is on mobile. If your thumb-reach zones are off, your conversion is off.

By the end of this phase, you receive a **Prioritized Growth Roadmap**. This isn't a "nice-to-have" list; it's a "must-fix" engineering document.

## 3. PHASE 2: CRO & REVENUE PSYCHOLOGY
If Phase 1 was about fixing the plumbing, Phase 2 is about maximizing the flow. Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) is often misunderstood as "changing button colors." At **The Solian Wolves**, we view CRO as the scientific application of user psychology to technical environments.

### 3.1 The Hierarchical Testing Framework
We don't test random ideas. We follow a strict hierarchy of optimization:
1. **Functional (Is it broken?)**: Before testing a new layout, we ensure the existing one works on every device/browser combination.
2. **Accessible (Can everyone use it?)**: We ensure the site is usable for people with disabilities, which inherently improves the experience for everyone.
3. **Usable (Is it easy?)**: We simplify the "Critical Path" (the shortest route to checkout).
4. **Intuitive (Is it obvious?)**: We use design patterns that users already recognize.
5. **Persuasive (Does it sell?)**: Only here do we begin testing copy, social proof, and scarcity triggers.

### 3.2 The Psychology of the "Buy"
We implement and test specific psychological triggers across your site:
- **Heuristic Evaluation**: We audit for "Decision Fatigue." If you give a user 5 choices, they choose nothing. If you give them 1 clear path, they take it.
- **The Zeigarnik Effect**: We use progress bars in checkouts. Humans have an innate desire to finish what they've started.
- **Loss Aversion & Scarcity**: We don't use fake countdown timers. We use real-time inventory tracking and "High Demand" indicators that are backed by actual server data.
- **Authority & Trust Signals**: We don't just "put logos on a page." We place trust badges at the "Point of Highest Friction"—usually right next to the "Pay Now" button.

### 3.3 Continuous A/B Testing Regime
We utilize professional-grade testing suites (Evolv, Kameleoon, or custom-built engines) to run split tests. Our process:
- **Hypothesis Foundation**: "By changing X, we expect Y to happen, because of Z."
- **Statistical Significance**: We never call a winner early. We require 95%+ confidence levels and a minimum sample size before implementation.
- **The Winner's Loop**: Once a test wins, it becomes the new baseline, and we start the next experiment immediately.

## 8. THE KNOWLEDGE VAULT: Glossary & FAQ
We believe an educated client is a successful client.

### 7.1 Key Industry Terms
- **A/B Testing**: Comparing two versions of a webpage to see which performs better.
- **Bounce Rate**: The percentage of visitors who leave the site after viewing only one page.
- **Conversion Rate (CR)**: The percentage of visitors who complete a desired action (like buying a product).
- **Lighthouse Score**: A tool from Google that audits performance, accessibility, SEO, and more.
- **Liquid**: The templating language used by Shopify (which we are masters of).
- **SSR (Server-Side Rendering)**: A technique where the server generates the full HTML for a page before sending it to the browser.
